About Health-Smart Meals

Health-Smart Meals is an intelligent meal recommendation system focused on your health and well-being. We analyze your blood sugar and blood pressure levels along with your available ingredients to suggest personalized, health-optimized meals.

Our system helps you manage your health through proper nutrition by:

  • Analyzing your health metrics (blood sugar and blood pressure)
  • Suggesting meals that help balance your health conditions
  • Supporting your dietary preferences (vegetarian, non-vegetarian, vegan, eggetarian)
  • Creating single-day or weekly meal plans based on your available ingredients
  • Providing detailed cooking instructions with health benefit information

Whether you need to manage high blood sugar, control blood pressure, or simply eat healthier, Health-Smart Meals provides the guidance you need to make informed food choices.
